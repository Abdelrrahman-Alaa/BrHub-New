    FAQs

    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT EXPORTING

    How do I start exporting through your platform?

    Starting is simple! Register your company, complete your exporter profile with product information and certifications, then start receiving buyer requests. Our AI will automatically match you with relevant RFQs from qualified importers worldwide.

    Is there a fee to use the export platform?

    You can create a profile, receive buyer requests, and respond to inquiries at no cost. We also offer premium plans with advanced features like priority placement, enhanced analytics, and dedicated support for exporters looking to maximize their reach.

    How do you verify buyers on the platform?

    We verify buyer companies through multiple checks including business registration, contact information validation, and trade history. Verified buyers display a badge on their profile. However, we recommend conducting your own due diligence and following safe trading practices.

    What types of products can I export through the platform?

    We support exporters across all legal product categories and industries - from agriculture and food products to machinery, textiles, electronics, and industrial supplies. As long as your products comply with international trade regulations, you can list them on our platform.

    How quickly can I start receiving buyer inquiries?

    Many exporters receive their first buyer inquiry within 24-48 hours of completing their profile. The key is providing detailed product information, competitive pricing, and quality certifications. Premium members with verified status typically receive inquiries 3x faster.
